About The Event

Shri Vaishnav Vidyapeeth Vishwavidyalaya, a prestigious university based in Indore (M.P.) is organizing Void Hacks(), a national level hackathon to give deserving students a platform to enhance and build something bigger than themselves. VOID HACKS() 6.0 is a 24-hour hackathon fostering innovation and creativity through learning by doing. It is to serve as a significant step towards a dexterous, technologydriven future led by constructive, problemsolving mindsets for the next generation.
